## Executive Summary

Penko Writer is a **free, open-source, privacy-first** word processor that rivals Microsoft Word and Google Docs while offering unique features neither competitor provides.

### Key Differentiators:
- **100% Free** - No subscriptions, no ads, no premium tiers
- **100% Private** - All data stored locally, no cloud required
- **100% Offline** - Full functionality without internet
- **Open Source** - Community-driven, transparent development
- **PWA** - Works on desktop, mobile, and tablet
